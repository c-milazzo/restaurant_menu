Havana Bites

A newly opened Cuban restaurant, is the realization of Chef Sofia Rodriguez's dream to bring the authentic flavors of Havana to the heart of the city. Inspired by her Cuban heritage and family recipes Sofia crafted a menu that celebrates Cuba's rich culinary traditions, offering dishes like Ropa Vieja, Arroz con Pollo, and more. The staff, led by Chef Sofia, is dedicated to creating a cultural and flavorful experience for diners. Located in Center City, Havana Bites exudes the warmth of a Cuban paladar with its cozy ambiance, vintage decor, and live music. The restaurant's primary goal is to share the essence of Cuban cuisine, using the finest ingredients and offering a cultural exchange where guests can savor not only the food but also the music, art, and hospitality that define Cuban culture. Come join us at Havana Bites, where every bite is a bite of Havana's soul.
